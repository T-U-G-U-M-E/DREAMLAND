Dreamland High School is a leading educational institution committed to excellence in teaching and learning. We are always looking for passionate, qualified individuals to join our team and contribute to our mission of providing quality education.

Current Openings

Mathematics Teacher

Department: Sciences

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics or Education
  • Teaching qualification (PGCE or equivalent)
  • Minimum 3 years teaching experience
  • Registered with Ministry of Education

How to Apply: Send CV and cover letter to dreamlandhs@gmail.com

Application Process

  1. Submit your CV and cover letter to dreamlandhs@gmail.com
  2. Include the position you're applying for in the subject line
  3. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews
  4. Successful candidates will undergo background checks
  5. Offer letters will be sent to successful applicants

Note: Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted. Dreamland High School is an equal opportunity employer.
